What is NuoLingo?

If you travel abroad without studying the local customs beforehand, you may get into some awkward situations.
NuoLingo prevents this.
Users can study flashcard sets, test themselves, and track their progress just with a few clicks.

Time saver

Live local without having to be local. Users have access to customs they won't find in a travel guide or pamphlet.

Confidence, not grades

We won't grade on you on how well you do. Rate how you think you did after every session, and get charts made just for you. Watch yourself grow!

Friends & Forums

Send your friends the newest deck and maybe one you thought was helpful. Chat about it with other users in our forums. They won't bite, we swear.

Learn Anywhere

Wherever there's a computer, there's a way. Register for an account, save your progress, and practice any time.

Language for all

Don't have a smartphone? We got you covered. Check out our sister app Dialect. It's a fully featured SMS version of NuoLingo. Cool, right? (The answer is yes.)
