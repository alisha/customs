What is NuoLingo?

If you travel abroad without studying the local mannerisms and traditions beforehand, you may find yourself getting into tricky and uncomfortable situations. NuoLingo prevents such things from happening. Users are able to study flashcard sets, perform evaluations and track their progress in understanding these foreign concepts.
